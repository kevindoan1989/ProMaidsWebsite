Why Work In Singapore?

Singapore is a safe place to work in, with laws to protect both citizens and foreigners (including maids) who come from abroad to work here.

Our country is a multi-racial city that welcomes people of all cultures. Due to the close and long-standing relationship between Singapore and Malaysia, you will find similarities in terms of culture and food – this will help you settle down faster.

Because of the close proximity of our countries, many Malaysians find it extremely convenient to come here to work. Singapore is a small but beautiful city, boasting majestic sights of an urban jungle that’s worth working in and visiting.

Singapore has a good standard of living, and is a secure place to work in. Our Ministry of Manpower has rules in place that employers must follow to care of you during your employment.
